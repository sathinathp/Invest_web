The Conflict

Why this is hard to find

01 / PORTFOLIO PRESSURE

Most VCs manage large portfolios

Standard funds are designed to play a numbers game. If a setup doesn't hit hyper-growth velocity instantly, attention shifts, priorities change, and support fades away.

02 / ANGEL LIMITS

Most Angels lack the ticket size

Individual angel investors have valuable operational backgrounds but lack the institutional capital runway, legal infrastructure, and co-investment capacity you need.

The Solution

So Lemniscate is built differently

We align our personal wealth and networks directly with each founder. We don't run a "spray-and-pray" game. Each setup company receives dedicated legal, financial, and capital routing resources from our active partners.

Milestone 1

We make a small number of deliberate co-investments per year. Each deal is fully backed by our personal capital.

Milestone 2

Setup Engineering

We guide you through core entity registration, banking access, and infrastructure onboarding to speed up deployment.

Milestone 3

Continuous Runway

We show up on board meetings, during rebalances, and help secure follow-on growth rounds through our LP database.
